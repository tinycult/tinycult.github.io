How Does It Work?

Being a Creator with Tiny Cult is simple. We partner with brands across the UK and work with them to understand what photos and videos they need. We handle all the admin so you can just focus on the fun part - making great content!

Receive Products

Once you're accepted, you'll be sent products from our clients. It could be anything from health products to beauty, gifts, food & drink or gadgets.

Create Photos & Videos

You'll receive a set of guidelines explaining what photos or videos need creating. Typically it involves demonstrating how a product works or showing the unboxing experience.

Submit & Get Paid

Once you're done simply send your content back to us. We'll check it and then you'll be paid promptly. Plus, you get to keep all the products for free!
